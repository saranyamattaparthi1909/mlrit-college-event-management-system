TechnoFest 2025

About the Event

TechnoFest 2025 is MLRIT’s annual national-level technical symposium featuring a wide range of workshops, coding contests, paper presentations, robotics challenges, and project exhibitions. It is an exciting opportunity for students across the country to showcase their technical skills and creativity.

Event Details

Date: 15th September 2025

Time: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Venue: Auditorium, Block A, MLRIT

Schedule Highlights

  • 09:00 AM – Inauguration Ceremony
  • 10:30 AM – Keynote Session on Emerging Technologies
  • 12:00 PM – Coding Hackathon Begins
  • 02:00 PM – Paper Presentation Competition
  • 04:30 PM – Robotics Challenge Finals
  • 05:30 PM – Prize Distribution
